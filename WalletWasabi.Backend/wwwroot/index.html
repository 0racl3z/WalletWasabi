Frequently Asked Questions

Why did my enqueued coins get banned?

First you disrupt a round (not following up in a phase other than Registration) your output gets noted. Next time you do this your output gets banned. This can happen due to various reasons:

- you are DoS attacking the coinjoins
- you lost internet connection at the wrong time
- an intermediary Tor node lost internet connection at the wrong time
- you killed the software at the wrong time
- your software crashed the wrong time
